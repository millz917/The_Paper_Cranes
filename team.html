Who are we?

We are proud to say that The Paper Cranes is only possible thanks to the hard work and dedication of our team members and contributors. The Paper Cranes started as a small group of friends interested in origami, which now grown into a team of 9 core members and a large number of anonymous contributors. Our mission is to spread the positive benefits of origamis among students and to promote different cultures through the art of paper folding.
